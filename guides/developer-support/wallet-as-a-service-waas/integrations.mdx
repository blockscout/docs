## Current Integrations

### Rootstock

Rootstock is the first live WaaS integration, made possible by a [grant from the Rootstock Collective DAO.](https://gov.rootstockcollective.xyz/t/2512-grant-1-rootstock-global-wallet-by-blockscout/679https://gov.rootstockcollective.xyz/t/2512-grant-1-rootstock-global-wallet-by-blockscout/679) The wallet provides coverage for the entire ecosystem with a simple login, swap, onramp, and account abstraction features.

- Rootstock Global Wallet  https://rootstock-wallet.blockscout.com/

### Polygon

A standalone wallet is available for the Polygon ecosystem. Swap, deposit and send tokens seamlessly.

- Polygon Global wallet https://polygon-wallet.blockscout.com/
